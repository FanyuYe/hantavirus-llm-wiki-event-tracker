## Key Claims Used

- Three people, including one British national, with suspected hantavirus had been evacuated from the Hondius to receive medical care in the Netherlands.
- Remaining British nationals could be repatriated once the ship docked at its next destination if they did not develop symptoms.
- UKHSA said none of the British citizens onboard were reporting symptoms at the time of this update, but they were being closely monitored.
- UKHSA was aware of two people who returned independently to the UK after being on board MV Hondius; neither was reporting symptoms, and both were advised to self-isolate.
- UKHSA was supporting a small number of close contacts of people on the boat; none were reporting symptoms.
- UKHSA said the risk to the general public remained very low.
- UKHSA and government partners were tracing further individuals who might have been on the same flight as a confirmed case, to perform public-health risk assessments and precautionary measures.
