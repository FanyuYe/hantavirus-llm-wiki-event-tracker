## Summary

Western Australia Department of Health source card supporting the Hondius hantavirus timeline and daily report.

## Key Claims Used

- WA Health said it was supporting the Australian national response to repatriation of Australians after the MV Hondius hantavirus outbreak.
- The Australian Government advised that returning Australians and one New Zealander would arrive in Perth late on 2026-05-12 at RAAF Pearce Air Base.
- The returning travellers were to be securely transported to quarantine at Bullsbrook National Resilience Centre.
- WA Health said the Australian Government would seek further advice from the Australian Health Protection Committee about further quarantine arrangements.
- The Australian Government confirmed that none of the passengers being repatriated to Australia were displaying symptoms.
- WA Health said travellers would be escorted by appropriate personnel wearing personal protective equipment.
- WA Chief Health Officer Dr Clare Huppatz said the rare illness presented no risk to the community given the quarantine provisions.
- WA Health said the Bullsbrook National Resilience Centre included onsite medical facilities.
