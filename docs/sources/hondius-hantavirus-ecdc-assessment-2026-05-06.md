## Summary

ECDC threat assessment and recommendations for the cruise-ship-associated Andes hantavirus cluster.

## Key Claims Used

- ECDC reported seven cases as of 2026-05-06, including three deaths, one ICU patient, two symptomatic people aboard, and one case diagnosed after return to Switzerland.
- ECDC said two samples tested positive for hantavirus by PCR and one additional sample tested positive for Andes virus by PCR.
- ECDC considered all people aboard close contacts as a precaution because of the closed setting.
- ECDC assessed the risk to the EU/EEA general population as very low.
